The two incarnations of the seven emotions that were similar to Goddess Houtu, the love of the seven emotions and the joy of the seven emotions, were bowing to the Goddess of Golden Spirit together. They smiled and said, “Thank you.”

Jin Ling shook her head slightly and said, “Take care.”

“Yes.” They agreed softly. Their figures slowly rose into the sky and flew towards the Lady of Earth. In the sky, at the Great Daoist Master’s residence. “Just you wait!” The little evil woman gritted her teeth. “When I come out again, I will definitely shave your hair and carve a green-haired turtle on your head!”

The Great Daoist Master smiled and shook his head. He secretly crushed the pearl in his hand and retrieved the Taiji Painting. A beam of golden light shone over and wrapped around the indignant little evil woman before slowly pulling her towards Goddess Houtu in the sky…

In another corner.

“Excuse me.”

Zhao Gongming bowed to the incarnation of “fear” in front of him. He took off his mask and smiled apologetically. The incarnation of “fear” that was already restrained by the golden light heaved a sigh of relief. It made a ferocious face at Zhao Gongming, as if it was responding to the dozens of times it had been frightened.

In another corner, the incarnation of anger, which was also restrained by the golden light, cursed at Perfected Primordial Unity below. “Just you wait! Bastard! I’ll hit you every time I see you in the future!” The bruised and swollen Perfected Taiyi smiled evilly. He lowered his head and spat out a mouthful of blood. His aura was weak.

When the nine immortals stepped into the small world, Perfected Yu Ding retracted his jade tripod and stood up to bow to the incarnation of desire. He said softly, “Sorry.” “Pui!”

When the incarnation of desire was swept away by the golden light, it cursed and said things like “monsters” and “they are all monsters”.

Then, the immortal senses of the immortals headed towards the last incarnation.

Golden rays of light descended like chains and gently helped the sad girl up.

“Ah…”

Little Grief sighed softly. She raised her hand and wiped the tears that had not stopped falling from her eyes. She looked at Li Changshou and Fairy Yun Xiao, who were standing in front of her, and said softly, “Look, I can’t go that far. Fake is still fake. I will be corrected if I’m wrong. I’m the one who is being corrected.”

Li Changshou smiled warmly and shook his head slowly.

As she muttered, she was slowly pulled up. “Although I know that you are not doing this for me, I… Thank you for the past few days. I’m a little talkative and useless… Why am I so miserable? I knew that it was this kind of fate from the beginning. It was because you guys had some thoughts that you shouldn’t have. However, in the end, you still had to do this. You clearly knew that this was the case… I know…”

Yun Xiao bit her lips gently and turned away. Li Changshou calmly looked at Little Grief and slowly exhaled. He smiled and said, “By the way, do you have a name?”

“I… I’m just a manifestation of the seven emotions of the Empress Earth. It’s a thought of hers.”

“I’ll keep calling you Little Grief in the future.” Li Changshou suddenly said in a clear voice, “My name is Changgeng. I’m a disciple of Ren School and the Heavenly Courts’ Water God. It’s my honor to be able to get to know you and be your Daoist. We can achieve what we have today and help the Lady of Earth!”

“I-I…”

Little Grief, who had been pulled into the air, kept wiping her tears with the back of her hand. She gradually lost her voice and kept shouting, “I am the Lady of Earth. I am the Lady of Earth who is bestowed by all living beings. My name is Houtu, the Lady of Earth… To be able to get to know you and become your Dao friend… I!”

Golden light flashed, and her figure disappeared with the other six incarnations.

Li Changshou slowly took a deep breath and looked down at the pearl that had fallen in front of him. He seemed to hear that sound…

I’m happy.

“Are you silly?”

Li Changshou picked up the pearl and said, “I tampered with it. Before you placed your sadness into this pearl, I melted it. It will always be pleasant.” A hand reached out and grabbed his arm. Li Changshou whispered, “Don’t worry. My Dao heart is very strong.” Yun Xiao agreed softly, but she did not let go of Li Changshou’s arm. In the sky, the golden light slowly dissipated. Seven different-colored pearls were circling around the Lady of Earth. Wisps of the power of the seven emotions returned to the Lady of Earth’s body and instantly neutralized each other. Suddenly, Houtu opened her eyes. The seven pearls around her exploded at the same time. The seven incarnations that could not resist appeared again. The Great Daoist Master frowned and said, “Senior Houtu, don’t…” “It’s fine.”

There was a faint glow in the Lady of Earth’s eyes. She looked at the seven incarnations that had lost most of their seven emotions and revealed a gentle smile.

She removed the straw hoop on her head and said softly, “I will bear the pain of all living beings. I will bear the grudges of all living beings. I will endure the hatred of all living beings. Why can’t I tolerate my own grief, evil, desire, joy, fear, love, and anger?” Great Daoist Master Xuan Du’s brows gradually relaxed. He smiled and said, “Senior, you can decide on your own. However, if you can’t suppress it next time, please inform me early.” “Yes, thank you.”

The Lady of Earth replied gently, her eyes filled with tenderness. Her slender fingers gently pushed the grass ring forward. The Seven Emotions incarnation transformed into streams of light and merged into the grass ring. The grass ring flew back into the Empress Earth’s arms and was held in front of her chest. “Be good in the future.”
